Who We Are

We are a laboratory specialized in developing advanced technological solutions for the hydrocarbon and water treatment sectors, with an innovative focus based on nanotechnology.

Vision

To be recognized for the efficiency of our industrial processes, applied research, design and operation of potable and wastewater treatment plants, and in the hydrocarbon sector, focusing on safety and respect for the environment.

Mission

To foster innovation in water treatment technologies, sustainable hydrocarbon extraction, and nanotechnology, generating efficient and responsible solutions that contribute to scientific progress, sustainable development goals, and environmental care.

Objectives

  • Develop nanotechnological solutions.
  • Optimize water treatment processes.
  • Promote environmental sustainability.
