Who We Are

We are a laboratory specialized in developing advanced technological solutions for the hydrocarbons and water treatment sectors, with an innovative approach based on nanotechnology.

Vision

To be recognized for efficiency in our industrial processes, in applied research, design and operation of drinking and wastewater treatment plants, and in the hydrocarbons sector with a focus on safety and respect for the environment.

Mission

To foster innovation in water treatment technologies, sustainable hydrocarbon extraction and nanotechnology, generating efficient and responsible solutions that contribute to scientific progress, sustainable development goals and environmental care.

Objectives

  • Develop nanotechnology solutions
  • Optimize water treatment processes
  • Promote environmental sustainability
